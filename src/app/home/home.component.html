Inspired by the late comprehensivist R. Buckminster Fuller, our mission is to make the world work for one-hundred percent of humanity. In order to accomplish this, we're starting with water.

Our society is built on top of a vast amount of infrastructure that serves our drinking and wastewater needs. And yet today, this infrastructure is crumbling - the Army Corps of Engineers gave American water infrastructure straight D’s.

Climate change is already a reality that threatens our water supply. Now is the time to integrate machine learning intelligence into our water infrastructure and increase the resiliency of our communities.

Water pumps are an essential part of water distribution and wastewater infrastructure. We’re using machine learning and predictive analytics to help you improve the performance of and spot problems with your pumps faster.

Control Your Risks

Aqueduct consumes live data on your pumps, and if it detects a problem, your engineers will receive real time alerts and help them decide to take action or not.
